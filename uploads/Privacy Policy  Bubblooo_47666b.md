# Privacy Policy

Bubbl.ooo is owned and operated by [172 Tech](https://172tech.com/)

## Information We Collect

When you use Bubbl.ooo, we collect information you provide directly: account details (name, email, phone), chatbot configuration data, training content you upload, and conversation logs between your chatbot and its users.

We also collect usage data automatically: IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, feature usage patterns, and performance metrics.

## How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and improve the Bubbl.ooo service, train your custom chatbot models (your data is never used to train models for other customers), send service-related communications, and comply with legal obligations.

## Data Storage & Security

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We host data on secure cloud infrastructure with SOC 2 compliance. Your training data and conversation logs are isolated per account.

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate Bubbl.ooo (hosting, AI model providers, payment processors) and when required by law.

## Your Rights

You can request data export, correction, or deletion at any time by contacting us at privacy@bubbl.ooo. We will respond within 30 days.

## Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of Bubbl.ooo after changes constitutes acceptance.
