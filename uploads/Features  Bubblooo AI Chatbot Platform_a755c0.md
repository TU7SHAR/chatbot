# Everything you need to win customers

From WhatsApp chatbots to lead generation, analytics to multilingual AI — Bubbl.ooo is the all-in-one platform for modern customer engagement.

💬

### WhatsApp-Native Chatbot

Deep WhatsApp Business API integration — automated flows, broadcast messaging, UPI/WhatsApp Pay payment collection, product catalogs, and smart follow-ups. Built for India's WhatsApp-first economy.

🌐

### Website Chat Widget

Embed a beautiful, customizable chat widget on any website. Works with WordPress, Shopify, Wix, custom sites — one line of code to deploy.

🧠

### AI Training on Your Data

Upload PDFs, Word docs, paste website URLs, or connect your knowledge base. Bubbl.ooo trains a custom AI model that knows your business inside and out.

🔀

### Visual Flow Builder

Drag-and-drop conversation flows with branching logic. Combine deterministic paths (lead capture, booking, FAQs) with AI for everything else. Best of both worlds.

🎯

### Lead Generation & Qualification

Automatically capture visitor info, score leads based on conversation quality, and route hot leads to your sales team. Integrates with popular CRMs.

📊

### Analytics & Insights

Comprehensive dashboard tracking conversation volume, resolution rates, customer satisfaction, popular questions, peak hours, and conversion funnels.

🤝

### Human Handoff

Shared inbox where bot conversations seamlessly transfer to agents. Full context preserved — no customer repeats themselves. Basic CRM built right in.

🗣️

### Multilingual AI

Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati + 50 more languages. Auto-detects visitor language. Uses Indian LLMs for regional cost efficiency.

🏷️

### White-Label Ready

Remove Bubbl.ooo branding for just ₹999/mo (not $199 like competitors). Custom domain support. Perfect for agencies and resellers.

🔗

### Integrations

Connect with Shopify, WooCommerce, Razorpay, Stripe, Zoho CRM, HubSpot, Google Sheets, Zapier, Slack, and more.

📋

### Vertical Templates

Pre-built bot templates for real estate, coaching institutes, restaurants, clinics, D2C e-commerce, and more. Go live in 10 minutes.

🔄

Training data automatically refreshes when your website or documents change. Your chatbot always has the latest info — no manual retraining.
